About KOLBOX

At KOLBOX, we bring back the feeling of Lebanese home cooking — the warmth of gathering around the table, the aroma of garlic and olive oil, and the recipes passed down from village to village.

Each box contains fresh, carefully prepared ingredients, thoughtfully portioned and hygienically packed, so you can recreate authentic Lebanese meals with ease and confidence.
